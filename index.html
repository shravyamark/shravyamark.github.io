Professional Summary

Shravya Markandeya is a Geographic Discipline Leader at Colliers Engineering & Design, leading the Highways & Traffic Engineering practice in the National Capital Region. Her diverse background includes traffic and ITS, transportation planning, project management, emerging infrastructure technologies, and data analytics. She has led teams on many regionally significant and critical infrastructure projects. She serves as an ambassador and a social thought leader for the Harvard Business Analytics Program and is a member of the Harvard Business Review Advisory Council.
